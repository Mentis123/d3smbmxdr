03 — Readiness Assessment

The Philosophy of Readiness

Readiness is a 2‚Äì4 week engagement measured in weeks, not months. Its purpose is singular: clear the path for delivery. Everything else runs in parallel.

‚úï  Not This

"Is everything in place?"

The traditional approach stalls on perfection. Organisations spend 12 months developing a data classification framework before deploying a single agent. Time to governance kills momentum and value.

‚úì  This

"Is anything stopping us?"

Identify showstoppers ‚Äî things that will damage your business if not addressed before moving forward. Mitigate those. Everything else is remediation in parallel.

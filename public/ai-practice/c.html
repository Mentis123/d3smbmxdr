Portfolio Artefact A

AI Portability.

Build once. Run anywhere. Move anytime.

One AI workload, any plane — public cloud, private data centre, or edge — with portability baked into the architecture, not bolted on. The customer keeps control of where intelligence lives, what it costs, and who can turn it off.

The three planes

Why portability

Four customer levers
01

Cost

Move workloads to where the economics win today. Training in public cloud burst, steady-state inference on owned infrastructure, edge where bandwidth is the tax.

02

Security

Keep regulated data on the plane your compliance officer can walk to. Move inference to the data, not the other way around.

03

Control

Own your intelligence. No single vendor can raise the price, throttle the API, or turn it off. Every workload has an exit.

04

Sovereignty

When the data must stay in-country, in-tenant, or in-building — the architecture already knows how to handle it without a rewrite.

"We need to be able to build once and have that portability. Part of our source needs to be our architecture and approach and how we deliver this stuff to allow complete flexibility for the customer." — Matt Gooden, 15 April 2026

Portfolio Artefact B

Our methodology is not a waterfall.

Six phases. Iteration loops. Multiple entry points. One squad.

The AI Service Lifecycle is deliberately non-linear. Customers enter where they are, phases feed back into each other, and value is measured against a sharpening ROI ladder — not against the completion of a stage gate.

Three buckets, six phases

Minimum durations · Alenka-verified
Envision

Where is the value?

Minimum 3 weeks
Phase 01 Strategy & Roadmap · Phase 02 Ideate & Prove Value. Fifteen use cases flushed, top candidates with conceptual design.
Transform

How do we build it?

Minimum 6 weeks
Phase 03 Solution Design · Phase 04 Build & Deploy. Platform foundations plus one or two production use cases end-to-end.
Operate

How do we scale & realise?

Minimum 6 weeks
Phase 05 Adopt & Scale · Phase 06 Operate & Optimise. Rollout, change, MLOps telemetry, ROI realisation, feedback into the squad.

Enter where you are

Three customer archetypes
S
Enters at Phase 01

Starter

Early-journey customer. No strategy, no production use cases, mostly curious. The squad begins with vision and business case before anything gets built.

B
Enters at Phase 03 or 04

Builder

Has prototypes in pockets across the business. Needs enterprise architecture, platform consolidation, and the discipline to ship one thing properly.

X
Enters at Phase 05 or 06

Sustainer

Running AI in production already. Needs adoption at scale, agentic workforce management, ROI measurement, and the enhancement feedback loop back into design.

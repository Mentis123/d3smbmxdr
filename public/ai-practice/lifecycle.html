AI Service Lifecycle

Our methodology is not a waterfall. Six phases. Iteration loops. Multiple entry points. One squad. Six operational phases parented under three executive phases — Envision · Transform · Operate. Commercial clarity compounds phase by phase so you commit to production with clarity, not hope. The loop below is the delivery shape. The phase grid unpacks the activities. The capability ribbon further down is the commercial shape — five SKUs that chain across the lifecycle. Data#3 squads deliver; the capability is what you buy.

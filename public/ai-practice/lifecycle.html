AI Service Lifecycle

Six operational phases parented under three executive phases — Envision · Transform · Operate. Commercial clarity compounds phase by phase so you commit to production with clarity, not hope. The phase grid is the delivery shape. The capability ribbon below is the commercial shape — five SKUs that chain across the lifecycle. Data#3 squads deliver; the capability is what you buy.

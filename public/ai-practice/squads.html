Assumptions & model notes
  • Squad shape: 2 core roles @ 50% + 4 specialist roles @ 25% = 2.0 FTE of effort, covering 6 role archetypes (AI Engagement Lead, Solution Architect, AI Engineer, Functional Consultant + Change Lead / QA Lead / AI Strategy Lead specialists)
  • Sprint unit of sale: 10 business days (2 weeks). Each sprint delivers 20 person-days of blended capability. Factory Pods and other offerings are priced as a number of sprints.
  • Daily sell rate: $2,400 (Principal Consultant, $300/hr × 8). Blended across core & specialist for simplicity — split out per role in v0.2 if needed.
  • Daily loaded cost: fully burdened — salary + super + leave + training + overhead + bench allocation. Default $1,200/day (~$264k/yr per FTE assuming 220 working days) ≈ 50% of sell rate. This is a placeholder — tune with real Data#3 burden rates.
  • Sprints per year per squad: 22 realistic out of 26 theoretical. Gap absorbs leave, training, sales support, deal gaps.
  • Specialist sharing: specialists work 25% on this squad and 75% on other squads / pre-sales / other programs. In the multi-squad view, the same 4-specialist pool can serve up to 4 squads before additional hiring kicks in — this is the scale-economics story.
  • Not modelled (yet): sales & marketing cost, tooling / platform costs, AI infrastructure pass-through, delivery risk / rework, pre-sales discovery effort, ramp-up time for new hires, partner margin pass-through.

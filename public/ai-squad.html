02 — Why it keeps happening

The delivery model is the real bottleneck.

It isn’t the model. It isn’t the data. It isn’t the appetite. It’s how the work is staffed and shipped.

A

Fractional staffing.

Half-allocated people own nothing. They context-switch between four engagements and rebuild rapport every standup. You think you’ve bought a team. You’ve bought a roster.

B

Tools moving faster than people.

The frontier resets every quarter. Internal teams can’t train fast enough to stay current and ship at the same time. They pick one. Usually neither happens well.

C

Patterns locked in heads.

The integrators that have shipped agentic systems carry the patterns in their heads. When they leave, the patterns leave. You’re back at week one for the next initiative.

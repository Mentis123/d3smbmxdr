03 — Platinum Accounts

The Platinum Account Concept

Leadership is asking real questions about Platinum Accounts. The concept is simple — the highest-value customers get a differentiated experience. The problem is: without a unified view, you can’t identify them.

★

Threshold Logic

Platinum qualification based on total cross-LOB revenue exceeding $2M annually with engagement across 3+ LOBs and blended margin above 35%.

✓

Benefits Package

Dedicated account executive, quarterly business reviews, priority escalation paths, early access to new services, and custom SLAs.

!

Why It Doesn’t Exist Yet

No system today can calculate total customer value across LOBs. Each practice sees its own slice. Platinum candidates are invisible.

→

How Virtual PnL Enables It

The Virtual PnL agent aggregates revenue, margin, and deal count across all LOBs — making Platinum identification automatic and real-time.

Urgency: Leadership is actively asking questions about Platinum Account criteria. This isn’t theoretical — they want to move on this. The Virtual PnL is the prerequisite.

05 — Agents

The Agent Roster

Six specialised agents, each with a defined role, working together through the orchestration layer to deliver the Customer Virtual PnL.

Agent 01

Data Harmonization

Ingests and normalises data from Kimble, Salesforce, Finance, and MXDR billing. Resolves entity matching across systems — ensuring "Acme Corp" in Kimble is the same as "ACME Corporation" in CRM.

Agent 02

Virtual PnL Calculator

Aggregates revenue, cost, and margin across all LOBs for each customer. Computes blended margin, weighted revenue, and total relationship value in real-time.

Agent 03

Platinum Qualification

Evaluates customers against Platinum thresholds: total revenue, LOB breadth, margin quality, and growth trajectory. Flags new qualifiers and at-risk downgrades.

Agent 04

Deal Orchestration

Monitors cross-LOB deal dependencies. If a BA deal is at risk, alerts the MXDR team if their revenue is coupled. Coordinates billing and delivery across practices.

Agent 05

Forecasting

Builds unified customer-level forecasts combining pipeline data from every LOB. Identifies revenue concentration risk and models scenarios for churn, expansion, and cross-sell.

Agent 06

Revenue Recognition

Tracks revenue recognition status across LOBs. Ensures cross-LOB deals are correctly attributed, margin is reported at the customer level, and no revenue falls between the cracks.

09 — Operationalisation

The Operationalisation Answer

The challenge is direct: “This isn’t an operationalisation problem — it’s that we haven’t operationalised anything yet.” Here’s how the Virtual PnL changes that.

Governance Framework

Clear ownership model: each agent has a designated Practice Manager as business owner. Quarterly reviews against defined KPIs. Escalation paths for cross-LOB disputes.

PM Incentive Alignment

Introduce cross-LOB incentive metrics alongside existing LOB targets. PMs rewarded for customer-level outcomes, not just practice-level margin. Virtual PnL provides the measurement layer.

Change Management

Phased rollout starting with 5 pilot accounts. Champions in each LOB. Fortnightly show-and-tell sessions. The dashboard sells itself once PMs see their customers in full.

Measurable KPIs

Cross-LOB deal count (+30% target Y1). Platinum account identification rate. Blended margin improvement. Time-to-quote for cross-LOB opportunities. PM adoption rate.
